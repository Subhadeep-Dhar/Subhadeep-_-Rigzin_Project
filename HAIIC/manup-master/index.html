About Healthcare Informatics and Analytics

Healthcare informatics :

Healthcare informatics techniques use information technology to organize and analyze health records in ways that lead to improved healthcare outcomes. Healthcare informatics is used by nurses, doctors, administrators and researchers in the healthcare industry, and training is available through associate's, bachelor's and master's degree programs, as well as certificate programs. Continue reading for details about various healthcare informatics systems and training programs.

Healthcare analytics :

Health care analytics is a subset of data analytics that uses both historic and current data to produce actionable insights, improve decision making, and optimize outcomes within the health care industry. Health care analytics is not only used to benefit health care organizations but also to improve the patient experience and health outcomes.
