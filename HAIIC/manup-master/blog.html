Info


We are pleased to announce that the International Conference on Healthcare Informatics & Analytics will be held in SMIT, Sikkim on July 05-09, 2023. The HAIIC aims to bring together outstanding researchers and students to exchange and share their experiences and researches of Medical and Health Sciences. We also provide the keynote address and several plenary invited speeches for discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns, practical challenges encountered and the various solutions in the fields of medical and health informatics.


Topics for submission

   - Social determinants of            Health/Health equity

   - Digital health Technologies

   - Drug development

   - Aging population and long      term care

   - Healthcare Management &      Technology Adoption

   - E-learning in Healthcare      settings

   - Diesease-based or Specific      Cleanical issues

   - Health Information Safety &      Security


Guidlines

         1. Paper Size: US Letter format (8.5" x 11").

         2. Paper length should not be more than 6 pages, including figures,      tables & references.

         3. A maximum of two additional pages are permitted, with an      additional charge of INR (₹) 1000 per page (Indian author) and $ 50      per page ( International author), to be paid during author      registration.

         4. Paper Formatting: double column, single spaced, 10pt Times      New Roman font.

         5. Margins: Left, Right, and Bottom: 0.75" (19mm).

         6. The top margin must be 0.75" (19 mm), except for the title page      where it must be 1" (25 mm).

         7. File Size Limitation: 5.0MB.

         8. No page numbering on the manuscript is allowed.


General Instructions

     1. All fonts must be embedded into the PDF file.

     2. All submitted papers will be reviewed by Technical Program      Committee and assessed on the basis of originality, significance,      technical soundness, and clarity of expression.

     3. The decision regarding the acceptance of paper is at the      discretion of Technical Program Committee.

     4. Authors of accepted papers will be notified 5th September 2023      onwards, and registration till 15th September 2023.

     5. The review committee may recommend rejection of final papers      that do not take account of reviewers’ requirements and      expectations in an acceptable manner.

     6. The conference chair reserves the right to remove papers from      the conference proceedings that do not meet the requirements.

     7. Authors should submit their original unpublished work to HAIIC.
